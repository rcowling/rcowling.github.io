The Rising State of College Tuition

This longform infographic storymap was created for an assignment in an advanced cartography course at UW-Madison. The infographic shows how tuition has constantly risen in the United States even faster than the rate of inflation. The maps were increated in Indiemapper and the infographic was created using Adobe Illustrator.
